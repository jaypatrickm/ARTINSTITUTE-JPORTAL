Mobile

Project

Pass It On - 2009 www.passiton.com

Challenge

Create a new mobile app that emphasizes in user interface design.

Solution

Pass It On is a mobile application for buying, selling, trading, your own items. You sign up into a social network which is connected by adding friends; this creates a network within your friendships and location. Through this network, you are able to take pictures of items you are trying to sell or get rid of, and upload them onto your account, which will then be displayed to your friends, and friends of friends. The demographic is 18 years and older. The design is sleek and minimal to keep things easy to maneuver and maintain clarity
