Project

Octave Management Group - 2011

Challenge

Create a new brand for Octave Management Group. A music management company specializing in up and coming music artists and entertainers. Must create a logo, business cards, envelopes, letterheads, ads. Demonstrate good uses and bad uses.

Solution

Used metallic tones of purple, teal, and orange, to create a logo that is shiny and pops out. The slants of the logo name creates a sense of movement and excitement. Moods that represent what Octave Management Group can create for you. The slants were included in the letterhead and envelope to keep an overall cohesive look. The business cards reflect the concert stage where their customers want to be. The styleguide is created within a website that describes the steps you need to use the brand.
